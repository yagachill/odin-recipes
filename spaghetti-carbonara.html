Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

This is a favorite supper dish that's quick and easy to make. I double it for our family of 4 growing boys.

We serve it all up and then add on the goodies that are left in the bottom of the bowl - there's always bacon and eggs left down there.

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 pound bacon, chopped
  • 4 eggs, well beaten
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

Directions

Step 1

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.

Step2

Meanwhile, place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown.

Drain, reserving some of the drippings, crumble and set aside.

Step3

Scramble eggs in bacon drippings.

step 4

Place spaghetti in a large bowl. Pour in olive oil, and mix well; use enough to just moisten spaghetti.

Stir in bacon, eggs, and Parmesan cheese. Serve immediately.

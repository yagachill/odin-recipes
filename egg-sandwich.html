Tom's Scrambled Egg Sandwich

Description

A dad came up with this recipe years ago. Over the years, everyone I have shared this with always reacts the same way.

First, they tell me 'that sounds awful!' After they eat it their story changes to 'that was amazing!'

I know the idea of mayo and mustard on a scrambled egg disk sounds odd, but trust me; you'll love it.

I know the cooking method for the eggs may be sacrilege for some folks; I just ask that you try it before changing it up."

  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 slices bread, toasted
  • 1 teaspoon prepared yellow mustard

Directions

Step 1

Beat eggs, milk, and salt together in a bowl.

Step 2

Heat a small nonstick skillet over medium heat. Cook eggs in hot skillet without

stirring until set on the bottom, about 1 1/2 minutes. Flip eggs and cook without

stirring until set on the other side, about 1 minute more. Remove from heat.

Step 3

Spread 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise on each piece of toast. Spread mustard over

mayonnaise onto each piece of toast. Place eggs on one piece of toast and top with

remaining toast.
